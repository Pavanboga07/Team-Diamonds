Features

Everything you need to optimize your portfolio

📊

Live Market Prices

Fetches real stock prices with automatic fallback to curated demo data when markets are closed or API is unavailable.

🔢

Integer-Linear Solver

A custom backtracking engine finds every valid whole-share combination within your exact budget — not just a single recommendation.

📈

All Feasible Portfolios

See every valid allocation ranked by capital efficiency. Export results as CSV for further analysis in Excel or Python.

👁

Watchlist

Track your favourite tickers with live prices updating every 30 seconds. One click to load any combination into the optimizer.

📋

Run History

Every optimization is saved locally in your browser. Re-run with fresh prices or jump back to any past result instantly.

🔌

Developer API

Full REST API with live docs and try-it interface. Integrate the solver into your own apps with curl, JavaScript, or Python.

How it works

Three steps to an optimal allocation

01

Pick your stocks

Choose 2–6 ticker symbols from the popular list or type your own. Live prices are fetched immediately.

→
02

Set your budget

Enter your total investment budget and a maximum acceptable cash remainder. No magic guessing — you control the parameters.

→
03

Get every allocation

The integer-linear solver finds all valid whole-share portfolios, ranked by how efficiently they use your capital.
